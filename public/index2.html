About Us

PLW Education (PLW) is a US based company with branch offices throughout China that focuses on the successful United States placement of Greater China students entering middle school, high school and college. We have over 20 years experience and the knowledge and relationships to successfully place your son or daughter into the best possible school and living arrangement. In today’s ever changing education system, it is important to find a partner and guide who will provide you with a one stop solution which places you and the student in a position to succeed. Our company focuses on Quality, Best Fit, Safety and School Reputation as our core values. We understand parents want an environment which is safe, friendly and academically challenging.
